Join the Chamber

Joining our Chamber opens up a world of opportunities for your business. Whether you're a start-up, a growing enterprise, or an established company, our membership benefits are designed to support your success and help you thrive in today's competitive business environment.

Complete the form below to start your journey with us:

Membership Level

NP Membership (Non-Profit)

Cost: No fee

Benefits:

  • Access to networking events tailored for non-profit organizations.
  • Participation in specialized training and workshops focused on non-profit management and fundraising.
  • Inclusion in the Chamber's directory of non-profit organizations.
  • Opportunity to collaborate on community projects.

Bronze Membership

Cost: $100 monthly

Benefits:

  • Invitation to exclusive networking events.
  • Access to training sessions covering essential business topics.
  • Featured listing on the Chamber's website.
  • Discounts on Chamber-sponsored events.

Silver Membership

Cost: $250 monthly

Benefits:

  • Priority access to premium networking events.
  • Advanced training programs for business skills enhancement.
  • Prominent advertising space on the Chamber's website.
  • Special discounts and offers from Chamber partners.

Gold Membership

Cost: $400 monthly

Benefits:

  • VIP access to exclusive networking gatherings.
  • Customized training sessions led by industry experts.
  • Premier advertising opportunities on the Chamber's website.
  • Exclusive discounts, perks, and personalized services.
